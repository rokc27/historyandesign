Overview: Although less than half the age of Boeing, Airbus has risen to become the top airplane producer in Europe and Asia. Airbus started in 1970 in Blagnac, France by Roger Betellie and Felix Kracht. Airbus has factories in the UK, France, Germany, China, Spain, and even one in the US. Airbus is known for making the first fly-by-wire airliner, the A320, and the worlds biggest commercial aircraft, the A380.
